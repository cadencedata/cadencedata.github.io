Dynamic, distributed data is harder.

The complexity scales exponentially with multiple dynamic datasets, each of which must be periodically updated to reflect some data source. To handle this case, you need to manually or programmatically trigger data pulls to the appropriate database. Your use case might even involve joins or merges across multiple databases.

Build models, not infrastructure.

Cadence is a hub for all your dynamic, distributed data operations. Write kits — abstract operations on your data in any language you choose — then schedule jobs in our intelligent DAG scheduler to be deployed to remote instances as you choose. No need to learn and configure Airflow or other complex scheduling packages.

Choose tools that watch your back.

Cadence can infer implicit dependencies across these jobs and intelligently modify your schedule to avoid potential data corruption and maximize throughput. Cadence learns your jobs' write patterns and can notify you of any potential data problems it detects. Monitor the health and activity of all of your jobs and data targets through Cadence's interface.

Get a hassle-free API into your own data.

With Cadence, there's no need to include messy, meta-programmed SQL in your analysis scripts. Cadence lets you define SQL hooks for a specific job's data results. You can pull your data into your desired analysis environment with the hook you defined simply by hitting an API endpoint. Cadence even allows you to parametrize hooks for greater flexibility.
